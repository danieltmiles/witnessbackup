3. Cloud Storage Integration

Our app automatically uploads videos directly to cloud storage services you configure, including:

Important: Your videos are sent directly from your device to your chosen storage provider. We never have access to your videos or your cloud storage credentials. Your privacy relationship is with your storage provider, and their privacy policies govern how they handle your data:
